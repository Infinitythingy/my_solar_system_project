Milky Way

Solar System

The Milky Way is a barred spiral galaxy with a diameter of about 100,000 light-years. It contains billions of stars, including our Sun, and is home to our Solar System.

Kepler-90

An eight-planet system discovered by the Kepler Space Telescope, resembling our Solar System in structure with planets orbiting a Sun-like star.

TRAPPIST-1

A system with seven Earth-sized planets, three of which lie in the habitable zone orbiting an ultra-cool dwarf star

Proxima Centauri

The closest planetary system to us, featuring Proxima b, a potentially habitable planet orbiting the red dwarf star Proxima Centauri
